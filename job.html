Job


In BDFE, you can choose between 5 Job. Each job have a basic and an advanced Asterisk.
I really recommend focusing on one job (except if you want to spend a lots of money in the game).

Killing monsters let you gain exp for the job you have equipped.
When you level up, you receive AP (Asterisk Power). You can spend them in the Asterisk Section.

  • You'll need to level up other basic job to level 30 for unlocking red nodes on the basic asterisk
  • Once you reach level 80 on a basic job, you have access to a mission for unlocking the advanced job
  • After level 80 on basic job, each level up give you AP for both basic and advanced job
  • You'll be able to unlock every node for the basic job when you reach level 99
