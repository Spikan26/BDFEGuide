Job


In BDFE, you can choose between 5 Job. Each job have a basic and an advanced Asterisk.
I really recommend focusing on one job (except if you want to spend a lots of money in the game).

Killing monsters let you gain exp for the job you have equipped.
When you level up, you receive AP (Asterisk Power). You can spend them in the Asterisk Section.

  • You'll need to level up other basic job to level 30 for unlocking red nodes on the basic asterisk
  • Once you reach level 80 on a basic job, you have access to a mission for unlocking the advanced job
  • After level 80 on basic job, each level up give you AP for both basic and advanced job
  • You'll be able to unlock every node for the basic job when you reach level 99

Each Job have is own playstyle, here's a little preview :

Knight

High HP and resistance, Knight's role is to be targetted by ennemies and bait their attack away from the party.
The percentage of chance you have to be targeted is called AGGRO.
Knights generate aggro with some specials and skills, they also provide good boost to the party.

BlackMage

Strong magical DPS, Black mages can easily hit really high damage and target multiple ennemies at once.
Multi-target damage is reduce, except with CONCENTRATE
They provide to own or the party elemental buff or resistance.
Black mages are weak against physical attack and their spells come with long casting time, so be carefull.
You can protect yourself with MAGIC BARRIER and reduce cast time with FAST CAST.

Buff / Debuff List

WhiteMage

Healer and buffer, White mages take cares of the party by providing heal, buff, supply of BP and cooldown reduction.
They also revive and cleanse debuff.
All spells have casting time so timing is important to avoid killing yourself.

Thief

Fast physical DPS, Thief is all about auto attacks and speed.
They can dodge easily and attack really fast the ennemies with multiple damage at once

Ranger

Good debuffer, Rangers can remove enemies buff and inflict Poison.
Their debuff have short duration but Rangers have Debuff Extention
